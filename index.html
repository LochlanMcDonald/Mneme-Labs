How it works

No smart appliance required. StoveWatch retrofits any stove or oven with one tiny camera and a little computer vision.

STEP 1 📸

Snap the baseline

During setup, StoveWatch photographs your stove in its off state. That photo becomes its reference for what "everything's fine" looks like.

STEP 2 🔍

Compare every 5 minutes

A fresh snapshot is compared against every known off-state image. Lighting changes are normalized away — only real changes on the cooktop count.

STEP 3 📲

Get notified

A flame, glowing burner, or pot flips the state to ON and your iPhone gets a push — with a reminder if the stove stays on too long.
