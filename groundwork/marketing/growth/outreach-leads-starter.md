# Groundwork: starter lead list (US + Canada, broad B2B SaaS)

A hand-picked starting batch of recently launched B2B software startups from
Y Combinator's Summer 2025 (S25), Fall 2025 (F25), and Winter 2026 (W26)
batches. All sell software to businesses, so a security questionnaire or SOC 2
request is coming for them soon, which is exactly Groundwork's moment.

**Read this first.** These names were compiled from public YC batch roundups
(July 2026). Before contacting anyone, verify each on the official
**YC directory: https://www.ycombinator.com/companies** (search the name). The
directory gives you the current one-line pitch, the **exact website**, and the
**founder names** (often with LinkedIn), which is everything you need to reach
out. A few may have pivoted or renamed since the roundups.

## How to turn each row into a contact (repeat per company)

1. Search the name on **ycombinator.com/companies** to confirm site + founders.
2. Find the founder's email from name + domain using **Apollo.io** or
   **Hunter.io** (free tiers work at this volume).
3. Personalize with their YC one-liner and send the email/LinkedIn note from
   `outreach-templates.md`.

## Enriched top targets (founders + sites, ready to contact)

Researched from public sources (July 2026). Still confirm the founder and
email on the company site or LinkedIn before sending. Emails are not published

## Notes

  security company and already know this space. Good to know as a peer, not a lead.
- These are AI-heavy, which is fine: AI startups handle a lot of data and sell to
  businesses, so they feel security pressure early.

  (filter Industry = B2B, sort by most recent batch) and from Product Hunt's
  daily B2B launches. That is a repeatable weekly 20-minute task, or automate it
  with Apollo once you are ready.
