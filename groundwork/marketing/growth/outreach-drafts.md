## Sending checklist

- Confirm the name and email are right (a wrong name kills the email instantly).
- Send Tuesday to Thursday, morning in their timezone.
- Space them out; do not send all six from a brand-new domain in one minute.
- Log who you contacted and when, so follow-ups (from `outreach-templates.md`)
  go out on schedule: nudge at 3-4 days, breakup at about a week.
