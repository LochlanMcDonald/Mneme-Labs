# Groundwork: Product Hunt launch kit

Product Hunt is a one-day spike of high-quality traffic (founders, builders,
early adopters). Done well it drives sign-ups, backlinks, and social proof you
can point ads at afterward.

## The listing

- **Name:** Groundwork
- **Tagline (60 char max):** A free, tailored security plan for your startup in 5 min
- **Topics:** Security, SaaS, Developer Tools, Startups
- **Links:** groundwork-security.com (and the demo video)
- **Thumbnail / logo:** the new mark app icon (`marketing/logo/app-icon-512.png`)

### Description (the short pitch under the tagline)

> Your first enterprise customer asks for your security posture. Your insurer
> sends a questionnaire. An investor asks about data protection. Groundwork
> learns what you build and what you run, then gives you a prioritized,
> plain-language security plan: what to do this week, this month, and this
> quarter, and why. No security team required. Free to start.

## Timing & pre-launch

- **Launch day:** Tuesday, Wednesday, or Thursday. **Go live at 12:01 AM PT** -
  PH days run on Pacific time, and you want the full 24 hours to accumulate.
- **1-2 weeks before:** set up a **"Coming soon"** page on PH so people can
  get notified, and warm up your PH account (comment genuinely on other
  launches so you're not a brand-new profile).
- **Line up support quietly:** message friends, early users, and your network
  a day ahead. Tell them the date and that you'd love their honest feedback.
  Do **not** ask for upvotes: PH forbids it and will penalize you. Ask people
  to "check it out and leave a comment."

## Launch day playbook

1. **12:01 AM PT:** confirm it's live, post your maker comment immediately.
2. Send your "we're live, would love your feedback" note to your list and
   socials (LinkedIn, X, the new Instagram). Link straight to the PH page.
3. **Reply to every single comment** within minutes for the first few hours -
   engagement velocity is what PH's ranking rewards.
4. Post a couple of follow-up comments through the day sharing a specific
   detail (a stat from the knowledge base, a common questionnaire question
   Groundwork answers) to keep the thread active.
5. Cross-post the Reel/short demo to your socials pointing at the launch.

## Reply templates (adapt, don't paste verbatim)

- **"How is this different from a checklist?"** → "Checklists are generic;
  Groundwork tailors to your stack and sequences the work by priority, with the
  reasoning, so you know what actually matters first. And if you want, a human
  advisor reviews your specifics."
- **"Is it really free?"** → "The tailored plan is free, yes. The paid tier
  adds a human security advisor and audit-ready reports, but you get real
  value without paying."
- **"Do you store our data?"** → honest, specific answer about what is and
  isn't stored (keep it accurate to how the app actually works).
- **"We're pre-revenue, is this for us?"** → "Especially for you, the cheapest
  time to get security right is before you have customers and data to lose."

## After the launch

- Retarget the launch-day traffic and use the momentum as the backdrop for the
